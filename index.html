Meet Moksaa App

Moksaa is a community of believers that enables to deal with challenges of life by seeking and sharing knowledge from the rich Indian scriptures, that helps one grow spiritually and attain peace of mind in all times. Moreover, believers can get ‘Pooja’ done in any temple of India with just a few clicks.
